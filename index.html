Demon Slayer The manga is set in the Japanese countryside during the Taisho era . Once the main character - a young coal merchant Tanjiro Kamado - was forced to stay because of bad weather in the city where he was selling his goods. Returning to his home village, he discovers that his mother, younger sisters and brothers have died as a result of the attack of demons, in the existence of which Tanjiro refused to believe before. The only survivor after this incident is his closest sister Nezuko, who turned out to be infected with demonic blood and turned into a demon herself, but managed to retain part of her human consciousness. Not wanting to leave his sister in trouble, Tanjiro decides to become a demon slayer in order to find from these creatures a means to make Nezuko human again
